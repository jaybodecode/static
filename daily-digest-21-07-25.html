Executive Summary: Cybersecurity Advisory
Date: July 21, 2025

Today's threat landscape is dominated by actively exploited zero-day vulnerabilities affecting core enterprise software (Microsoft SharePoint), ubiquitous web browsers (Google Chrome), and third-party applications (MOVEit). These flaws allow for full system takeovers and large-scale data breaches, leading to significant operational disruption, financial loss, and reputational damage. Threat actors are also employing sophisticated social engineering to target key personnel with malware.

Critical Actions:
1. Prioritize Immediate Patching: Immediately apply patches for SharePoint and Chrome, focusing on CISA's Known Exploited Vulnerabilities list.
2. Verify Third-Party Security: Scrutinize the security of all third-party software and data transfer tools.
3. Reinforce Employee Awareness: Alert high-value targets, such as developers, to sophisticated, targeted threats.
